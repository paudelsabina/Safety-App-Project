What's inside

Built for real Nepal emergencies — works whether you're in Kathmandu, Pokhara, Chitwan, Biratnagar, Butwal, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur or Nepalgunj.

!

3-second HELP

Hold to confirm — sends your live GPS to trusted contacts and dials Police 100. Silent mode hides haptics.

📍

Live GPS sharing

Live trip link to your trusted circle plus the nearest police station and fire brigade, sorted by distance.

🩸

Blood donor finder

Local on-device registry. Filter by group (A+/O−/AB…) and distance. One-tap urgent SMS with map link.

＋

Offline first-aid

CPR, choking, bleeding, burns, earthquake. Bilingual cards that work without internet.

🎤

Voice trigger

Listens for "HELP", "Police", "बचाउ", "मद्दत". Hands-free safety when you can't reach your phone.

📳

Shake to HELP

Three vigorous shakes triggers an emergency. Tuned to avoid pocket false-positives.

📞

Fake call decoy

Realistic incoming-call screen lets you exit uncomfortable situations safely.

🚨

Siren & flashlight

Loud siren, strobe SOS flashlight, check-in timer — your full safety toolkit in one place.

🌐

EN + नेपाली
